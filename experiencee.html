Intern life

22 July 2024 - 13 September 2024

During my internship, I learned a lot. I had the opportunity to improve my video editing skills, design posters, and learn how to create engaging social media content. I interned in the Multimedia and System Department at G-Mart Corporation Sdn Bhd, which was conveniently located near my house. The team was very supportive, and my supervisor was incredibly friendly and encouraging. I also got the chance to attend a car racing event, as I worked for an automotive company.

Annual Grand Meeting, Jawantankuasa Sukan Pelajar

17 December 2024

Since I am currently in my final semester at UiTM, I also wrapped up my role as part of the Jawatankuasa Sukan Pelajar, where I contributed as a member of the multimedia team. One of the memorable events during my time there was the Annual General Meeting (AGM), where we were required to dress up as movie characters. For the event, I chose to dress as Moana, which was a fun and exciting experience. The AGM was packed with activities such as character casting, singing performances, and engaging games that brought everyone together. It was a great way to end my journey with the committee while creating unforgettable memories with my peers.
